

up:: [[The Colony]]

WWN factions have Treasure and Experience.

Experience is gained by completing faction goals. Experience is used to increase faction stats, allowing the faction to own more assets, and obtain more advanced assets.

Treasure is obtained every month and is used for asset management.

Magic is a preqrequisite for some assets. 

The idea is to have some meter the community needs to fill up. That meter is partially filled every time a group adventure is completed, a solo with some pre-reqs is completed, or the Progress Colony downtime activity is performed.

So perhaps Experience, Treasure, and Magic all have separate "next level" amounts.

## Experience
0/20

Scenarios add 1
Mssions add 3
Adventures add 6
Progress Colony can add 0-3 based on rolls. 
- This is either an Intelligence (operations-type) check, Wisdom (offering council), or Charisma (working with people) checks. Strength or Constitution checks might work for manual labor. Not sure how dexterity could fit into it; dex-based characters might not be able to contribute as much to this endeavor.

## Treasure
0/10

Scenarios add 2
Missions add 5
Adventures add 10
Progress Colony can add 0-3 based on rolls

## Magic
0/50

Donated Magic Items -- Previous Value x2
- Common, 2
- Uncommon, 4
- Rare, 8
- Very Rare, 16
- Legendary, 32

Spending Divine Favors, Contacts, and Lore
- 1:1 conversion rate
 - This represents research done, networking for, and divine inspiration in increasing the magic levels of the faction

Successful Arcana add 1.

## Additional Faction Turn

0/100

For every 100 downtime days spent across the entire server, The Colony gets an additional Faction Turn!

# Complications

1 - You've stepped on someone's toes who is either trying to accomplish what you are or who is in direct opposition of what you're trying to accomplish. This could potentially lead to a rival!
2 - A vital part to your operations and workflow has been disrupted. Perhaps the latest quarry shipment hasn't arrived and has been taken up by bandits! You must embark on a quest before you can participate in this downtime activity again.
3 - One of your ideas The Colony implemented has had detrimental effects and your reputation has been damaged! Try to do something to mitigate those social effects.
4 - Your efforts are not popular among the merchantfolk.
5 - Your efforts are not popular among the criminalfolk.
6 - Your efforts are not popular among the commonfolk.
