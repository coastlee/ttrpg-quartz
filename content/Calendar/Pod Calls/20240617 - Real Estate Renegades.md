## Back Pocket

**DSCR Loan**
- Debt Service Coverage Ratio
    - Service loans in commercial space
    - Is the debt serviced to a certain degree?
    - Lender swill vary
    - Income $3,000, Cost $3,000, DSCR = 1
    - 1.25 means 3,750
    - 0.75 means 2,250 rent fee
    - Programs / circumstances vary
    - More money you put down the less they feel they need to be carried (you have more equity in the deal)
        - a ton down (30%+) means they'll probably get 0.75
- DSCRs are typically higher interest by .5%, private lenders are .5% higher issue, too
    - Financing can be as important as deals; really important and having options
- Seller finance deals
    - Subject 2
    - Wrap-around mortgage
    - Right now rates are like 4, 4.5, 5%
        - People selling it probably have a 3%
