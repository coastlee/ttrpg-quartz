# Feat: Blindness (Character Flaw)

- *No sight;* automatically fail ability checks relying on sight. Only undone by Wish. Suppressed under transformation effects like Polymorph.
- Movement speed decreases by 5.
- Gain proficiency in Braille.
- *Heightened Senses;* Roll an additional d20 on Wisdom (Perception) checks relying on smell or- hearing and use the higher of the two rolls.
- While not defeaned gain the following 2 hearing based detection features:
    - *Spatial Awareness;* Sense objects within 5'. In combat increases to 30' and spells can target points in space within 30' as if seen. Doesn't identify objects, requires Perception or Investigation check for hidden things.
    - *Creature Sense;* Detect creatures within 30', unless they are behind full cover or hidden. A detected creature is considered seen if they attack you, you attack them, or to target with spells or abilities.
- *Vague Descriptions;* Limited to vague location descriptions when communicating sensed objects or creatures.
