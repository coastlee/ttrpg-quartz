# Character Description

## Backstory

### Romana's Major Secret:
She's the heir of a powerful corporate dynasty. Romana gave up her wealthy inheritance and fled her family's corporate empire, leaving behind a life of privilege and influence. The corp, a ruthless conglomerate with tendrils reaching into every aspect of society, wants her back to fulfill her duties as heir and secure their legacy.

But Romana's reasons for leaving run deep. She uncovered damning evidence of the corp's involvement in illegal experimentation on sentient beings, using them as unwitting test subjects in their quest for technological advancement. Unable to reconcile her family's crimes with her own conscience, Romana made the difficult decision to sever ties with her family and abandon her inheritance.

Romana's family, unaware of the true reasons for her departure, believe her to be a wayward child in need of discipline and control. The corp's relentless pursuit of her stems not only from their desire to maintain their grip on power but also from their fear of what secrets Romana may possess and the threat she poses to their carefully constructed facade of legitimacy.

She has to stay one step ahead of her family's enforcers and deal with the guilt and shame of her family's crimes.

### Occupation

Tech, has an alias to keep out of her family's sights.
