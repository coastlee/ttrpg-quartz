# Savage Worlds

First introduced to me via [Me, Myself, and Die]() - Season 1, I've finally found myself in a game. This is in the [[Crimson Labyrinth]] discord server where I'm playing [[Romana Verrill]].

Savage Worlds uses the concept of an **exploding dice**, and skills are die-sized base, most commonly ranging from a 1d4 to a 1d12.

It has a lot of different supplements and can be used to play a variety of different genres.
