# Wolves of God

This is a medieval game by [[Kevin Crawford]]. I've started playing a solo on the [[Mythic Game Master Emulator]] discord server with the following two characters:

 - [[Ioun Bennett]]
 - [[Osric of Steyning]]

 This is a strictly solo playthrough. I also used **The Adventure Crafter** for designing the first few scenes. I got stumped a little bit and [had to reach out to the community](https://discord.com/channels/517880617854304256/715960533307752559/1232916535635345428) for some assistance.
