# Dungeon Crawl Classics

Also known as DCC, Dungeon Crawl Classics uses "Funky Dice" (d3, d14, d16, d24, d30) instead of the "advantage/disadvantage" concept from D&D 5e. It's a little more focused on how TTRPGs used to be back in the 80s. [[Level 0 Funnels]] are a major part of this game.
