# Coastlee & Bruce: Session 6 — [[Stealing a Job]]

## Session Summary

> [!tldr] Stealing a Job
> Coastlee and Bruce learned of a [job](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131289453210648578 "job
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131289453210648578)") discussed of by one **[[Captain Thorne]]**. The man was not willing to share his job so... Bruce decided to [throw him through a window](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131295816938696745 "throw him through a window
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131295816938696745)") and take his job anyways. Here's [the job](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131297650638065725 "the job
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131297650638065725)"). Before setting off from **[[Neriton]]**, they went [shopping](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131274297919414412 "shopping
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131274297919414412)"). In their journeys, they discovered an orc settlement immediately outside of Neriton called [Baggichew](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131299055834767543 "Baggichew
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131299055834767543)"), which they avoided entirely at Bruce's recommendation. That was followed by these eerie [obelisks](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131299769982132284 "obelisks
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131299769982132284)") covered in carvings where they were attacked by some bug-shadow creature. The [path ended abruptly](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131318982159368232 "path ended abruptly
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131318982159368232)") and they had to get un-lost. In their search to find themselves, they, ironically, found a place that some go to "find themselves". A [monastery](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131327989028765807 "monastery
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131327989028765807)"). They learned of their leader's kidnapping and agreed to see if there was anything that they could do. Bruce and Coastlee continued their journey, only to stumble upon the [lair of an ogre and its pet eagle](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131331678397284433 "lair of an ogre and its pet eagle
(https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1131273739754016889/1131331678397284433)")... It's been a busy day.
