## [Introduction](https://discord.com/channels/1210327907285868564/1237460348047593574/1242954254516490322)

*Alpherat and Ellie have spotted signs of evil worship occurring in the halls of the recently reclaimed drow temple ruins that have been repurposed to [[Forseti]] and [[Tyr]].*

*They discovered that a small congregation on the outskirts of town have begun to worship [[Hel]], a goddess of death. In addition to that bit of news, they also learned that their congregation is built on an entrance to an 'underworld', though they have not yet figured out what that truly entails...*

They ventured to the location that Alpherat and Ellie had discovered, just outside of **The Colony**. Standing as sentries outside the ruins were two guards ([thugs](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/17035-thug?) with max hit points and spears instead of clubs). Both were slain, but not before one sounded an alarm! The group tried to hide their bodies, but the first wave of reinforcements barged through the doors before they could finish. 

This group was also handily defeated; it consisted of a [cult fanatic](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/16836-cult-fanatic) and several [dragonclaw](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/17470-dragonclaw) minions that only had 1 hit point each.
