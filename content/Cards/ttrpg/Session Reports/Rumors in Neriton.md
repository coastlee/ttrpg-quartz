# Coastlee & Bruce: Session 5 — [[Rumors in Neriton]]


## Session Summary

> [!tldr] Rumors in Neriton
> Many among the lips of those in taverns speak of a recent heist. The **culprit stole a cache of items** from a **merchant's warehouse** and has fled to a **swamp** where they were last seen. Intriguing both **[[Ankett]]** and Coastlee, they signed up for the hunt and were given a clue. The tracks left by the culprit made of flour. A special kind of flour that is coincidentally hard to remove. They successfully tracked down the thief, defeated them, and returned to **[[Neriton]]** to let **[[Marco Spencers]]** know that his goods have been recovered.
