# Kidnapping of Master Liang

After convincing the **[[Order of Harmonious Serenity]]** that they meant no harm, [[Coastlee of Neriton]] and [[Bruce Ankgar]] were divulged a terrible secret. The Grandmaster of the order, _**[[Master Liang]]**_, was the victim of a kidnapping. The mercenary group that took him has a fairly large lead and it may take some time to meet them. This quest will entail a series of quests that get the adventurers closer to actually saving the grandmaster with the early quests resulting in finding clues along the way.

## Part 1: Searching the Monastery
---

This quest entails a series of skill challenges as your party searches the monastery grounds for clues surrounding the kidnapping.

### Details
In order to satisfy this quest, your party must complete the following:  
- [ ]  Complete an **Easy Skill Challenge** that includes a **Persuasion check** with monks from the Order as you request access to the rooms of the grandmaster (the last known location of master Liang)

> _Failing this skill challenge results in requiring the adventurers to spar with the monks to gain their attention again, this counts as the resultant encounter. Lethal damage is fine as they'll do likewise but killing opponents who are **bleeding out** is heavily frowned upon. Another attempt at the skill challenge can not be attempted until the next day._

- [ ] Complete **two Medium Skill Challenges** that include either **Investigation** or Perception **checks** as you search through the rooms.

> _Failing these result in more sparring matches with the monks to regain their good graces. Once the sparring match is over, you are let back into the rooms to try again._

- [ ] Complete a **Hard Skill Challenge** including a **Persuasion** check to convince the monastery to let you take some of the evidence you will need further on in your quests.

> _Failing this results in a sparring match and you are unable to attempt the challenge again until the next day._

### Rewards
Completing this quest grants the following rewards in addition to the normal session rewards:

- 100gp worth of trade goods or 50gp worth of trade goods each, (whichever is higher).
- A set of Costume Clothes in the guise of a merchant for each member of the party.
- A hastily scrawled map

## Part 2: Following the Trail
---
This quest details the journey you must take, finding the right path, to the merchants hideout.

### Details

In order to satisfy this quest, your party must complete the following: 
- [ ] Continue travelling in the `Coastal` Terrain until you find a `Rocky Outcropping`. 
- [ ]  Start travelling in a `Hills` Terrain as the landscape changes accordingly until you find a `Small Mountain Range`. 
- [ ] Start travelling in a `Mountains` Terrain as the landscape changes accordingly until you find a `Canyon Gully`. 
- [ ] Complete a Hard Skill Challenge to traverse down the canyon gully to a structure hidden within.

> _A climbing speed counts as 1 automatic success towards this skill challenge_

### Rewards

Completing this quest grants the following rewards in addition to the normal session rewards:

- 100gp each
- 3 DtC of the parties choice split amongst the party.

#### Optional Rewards

- [ ] Rolling a 1d100 for encounters after every 10 miles (encounter on a 81+) will result in the following additional additional rewards:

- 400gp (or 200gp each (whichever is higher))
- 1x roll on the Magic Item Table A for each party member.

## Part 3: Infiltrating the Stronghold
---
This quest entails your parties efforts to enter the hideout, culminating in a final battle for the life of the grandmaster.

### Details

In order to satisfy this quest, your party must complete the following:
- [ ] Complete 2 Hard Skill Challenges to infiltrate the hideout and make your way to the inner sanctum. 
	- _Failed skill challenges must utilize humanoids in some way._ 
- [ ] Complete 1 Deadly Encounter vs some of the guards of the Merchant Leader, [[Escobar de Medici]]. 
- [ ] Complete a final Deadly Encounter vs Escobar de Medici himself. 
	- _Work with the DM/Validator team to flesh out Escobar to be a Rogue Sorcerer Multiclass of a challenge appropriate to a deadly encounter and have a member of the server staff pilot Escobar during the fight_

### Rewards

Completing this quest (and both preceding quests) grants the following rewards in addition to the normal session rewards:

- 150gp (each)
- 1 Upper Class Contact as you safely bring Master Liang home to the Monastery (each)
- 1 custom-built, soul-bound, tier appropriate item. To be built by a DM/Validator and approved by the DM staff. (awarded to either Coastlee or Bruce only)
