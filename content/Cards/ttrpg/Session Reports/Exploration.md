# Coastlee and Bruce: Session 2 — [[Exploration]]


## Session Summary

> [!tldr] 
> Bruce led Coastlee out of **Hillcrest** to flee the guards. Apparently even his standing at the tavern wasn't enough to protect him from sniveling, vindictive guards. So they started hoofing it through the jungle. They set off an ape the size of Bruce when they set up camp for the first night... Then a storm hit the second day. While they were traveling, they encountered **a nobleman's carriage** that was struggling against **bandits**. Bruce and Coastlee took care of the bandits, and the nobleman said to meet him in **Neriton** (with a possible quest?). They also ran into a raptor that day... constructed a primitive shelter to sleep through the night, and continued on the next day. They ran into R.O.U.S.'s, doubted their existence, pretended like they didn't exist, but... it's hard to argue with reality when they're gnawing on your shoulder. 🐀
