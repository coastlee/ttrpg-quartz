# Coastlee & Bruce: Session 4 — [[The Peryton of Neriton]]


## Session Summary

> [!tldr] _The Peryton of Neriton._
> Coastlee, Bruce, Ankett, and some other individual completed the [[Exploration Continued#Optional Quest [The Peryton of Neriton](https //discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1082110342613717042/1082854813299134484)|The Peryton of Neriton]]
> 
> [[Neriton]] is the largest town on a fjord known as [[The Nereid’s Sound]]. The sound is dotted by the country cottages of merchants and nobles from around [[Dominion Isle]], and [[Neriton]] is the main hub for their supplies and services. A lighthouse sits on an island a ways out from [[Neriton]]'s small harbour, an island most curious for its constant slow rotation. Recently, a monster known as a [[peryton]] took roost in the lighthouse and has been harassing the villas around the Sound. Though the monster has always been driven off when it attacked, several guards have lost their lives defending the villas. [[Aldreth Cenwulf]], the nobleman that [[Coastlee of Neriton]] and Bruce encountered earlier, has put out a bounty on the creature.
