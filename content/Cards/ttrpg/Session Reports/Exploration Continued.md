# Coastlee and Bruce: Session 3 — [[Exploration Continued]]


## Session Summary

> [!tldr] 
> Coastlee followed Bruce for many more miles to **Neriton** where they were supposed to have a job waiting from the nobleman they'd rescued on the second day of their journey. Coastlee started to doubt whether or not they'd ever arrive! On the third day of their journey, it was raining as usual, they came across an area be cleared of trees by kobolds. They'd been taking advantage of the cloud cover to get work done. Bruce and Coastlee only ran into a handful of them who were out foraging for food. When they set up camp that night, some plants attacked them... There must've been some spooky magic beyond of their comprehensions going on in the area. Unable to press on, though, they managed to sleep through the night. After _Neriton was finally insight_, they were attacked by some of the strangest creatures either of them had ever seen. They didn't know how to describe them. But they were freaky.

#### Optional Quest: [The Peryton of Neriton](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1082110342613717042/1082854813299134484)

[Source](https://discord.com/channels/733841578174447717/1082110342613717042/1082854813299134484)

[[Neriton]] is the largest town on a fjord known as [[The Nereid’s Sound]]. The sound is dotted by the country cottages of merchants and nobles from around [[Dominion Isle]], and [[Neriton]] is the main hub for their supplies and services. A lighthouse sits on an island a ways out from Neriton's small harbour, an island most curious for its constant slow rotation. Recently, a monster known as a [[peryton]] took roost in the lighthouse and has been harassing the villas around the Sound. Though the monster has always been driven off when it attacked, several guards have lost their lives defending the villas. [[Aldreth Cenwulf]], the nobleman that Coastlee and Bruce encountered earlier, has put out a bounty on the creature.

🔹 Roleplay meeting with Aldreth and discussing the Peryton. He explains the lighthouse and its peculiar rotation, and that at low tide one can walk across a rocky spit, through shallow water in some places, to reach it.
🔹 Complete a Hard Quest fighting off ornery sea creatures as you approach the lighthouse, culminating in a Deadly encounter including a peryton. Complete the next requirement before facing the peryton!
🔹 Complete two Fast Medium skill challenges to reach the lighthouse without the peryton noticing. On a failure, the peryton attacks while you are crossing the rocky spit.
